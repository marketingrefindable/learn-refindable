5 QUESTIONS
YOU NEED TO ANSWER

Sanjay Hegde (Caliber Labs — works with Atlassian, Amplitude, AirOps) didn't pitch anything. Just asked the questions most people are avoiding.

Are you building for humans or for agents?
Most of your customers will be running agents by next year. Onboarding 5 people + an agent stack is very different from onboarding 15 humans. Your product needs to handle both.
Cloudflare launched Markdown Mode — HTML for humans, converted to what agents prefer. Token usage down 80%. Both versions coexist.
Are you a feature or a system of work?
If your value is in displaying or reorganizing data that already exists, an AI can replace you. Proprietary logic, unique datasets, regulated flows — that's what makes you infrastructure.
Abridge: looks like a note-taking app. But deeply integrated into Epic EHR, suggests billing codes, connects multiple systems. Worth $5B. The note is commodity. The integration layer is the value.
Are you enabling your customers to win?
16% of teams redesigned for AI. 84% want agentic AI and plan to invest more. Your typical customer has no CTO. You are their AI strategy. Forward deployed engineers grew 8-10x in 2025.
Is your pricing aligned to repeatable outcomes?
This is the unique window when customers are open to that conversation. Correlate value to the work the company does — not the seat count. This moment won't last.
Are you learning fast enough?
Frontier labs double their benchmark scores every 7 months. Pick one internal metric and improve it 10% per month. What % of your product is used agentically? How long to onboard a new client?
